About Tech for Good

Tech for Good is a student-lead non-profit focused on enhancing social good in our society through the application of technology.

Our Mission

The mission of Tech for Good is to serve as a space allowing for utility and positive change for society through our projects. You can view our projects here.

Our History

In Fall 2023, we started with the idea of creating a space where social good and technology can intertwine. Since then, we've grown to become a group of students passionate about social good and technology. Since we've started, we've picked up over 170 pounds of trash, developed 3 projects, held 2 workshops, and have begun refurbishing hardware.
